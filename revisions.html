Revisions

I'm ecstatic to see the completion of my first website design. However, there is always room for improvement. I reached out to a few friends and family members to provide feedback on my site. These are my results...

Before:

This is my website before any revisions were made.

Feedback:

My friends and family members provided very helpful suggestions on grammer, flow of content, layout, color choices and website navigation features. Most of the feedback was positive but I did make the following changes to the original site. I fixed the grammatical errors, tweaked the spacing of the text and images so it flowed better on the pages, and finally with all the content on the design process page, I created additional links to assist the user in navigating to a certain spot on the page.

After:

I implemented all the suggestions from the feedback and this was the end result.
